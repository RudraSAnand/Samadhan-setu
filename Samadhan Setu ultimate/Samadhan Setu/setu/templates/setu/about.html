About Samadhan Setu

Empowering Citizens, Simplifying Governance. Samadhan Setu is India’s trusted digital bridge for civic engagement, grievance redressal, and transparent governance; enabling every citizen’s voice to create meaningful change.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To make grievance reporting seamless, tracking transparent, and resolutions accountable — establishing trust at every step of civic governance.

Vision

To set a new benchmark for citizen engagement by harnessing modern technology, design, and empathy-driven processes — unlocking a confident, trusting India.
