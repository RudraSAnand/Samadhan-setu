Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a grievance?+
Use the 'Report Grievance' button on our homepage, fill out the form with details and supporting images, and submit. Simple and quick!
How can I track my complaint status?+
Go to 'Track Status', enter your complaint ID or phone number, and follow the progress in real time.
Is my data secure?+
Absolutely. We only use your information to resolve grievances and keep you updated. We do not share your data with third parties.
Can I submit complaints anonymously?+
Currently, basic personal information is required for follow-up and accountability purposes.
